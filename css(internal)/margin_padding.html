Margin vs Padding Demonstration

📏 MARGIN - Space OUTSIDE the border

Margin creates space between elements. Background color does NOT extend into margin.

Margin: 10px (all sides)
Margin: 20px (all sides)
Margin: 30px (all sides)
Margin: 50px (all sides)

Individual Margin Properties

margin-top: 30px
margin-right: 50px (look right)
margin-bottom: 30px
margin-left: 50px

📦 PADDING - Space INSIDE the border

Padding creates space between content and border. Background color extends into padding.

Padding: 10px (all sides)
Padding: 20px (all sides)
Padding: 30px (all sides)
Padding: 50px (all sides)

Individual Padding Properties

padding-top: 30px
padding-right: 50px
padding-bottom: 30px
padding-left: 50px

Key Differences:

  • Margin is OUTSIDE the border - creates space BETWEEN elements
  • Padding is INSIDE the border - creates space AROUND content
  • Background color affects padding but NOT margin
  • Margin can have negative values, padding cannot
  • Margin collapses (adjacent margins combine), padding does not
